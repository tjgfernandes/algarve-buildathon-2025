Team Building / Rules

  • You can register as an individual or as part of a team (up to 3 people).
  • Solo registrations will form teams.
  • The organization may add team members (student or professional) to a max of 4 people, on the day of the event to encourage collaboration.
  • Both students and professionals are encouraged to join, diverse teams are strongly valued.
  • Teams own their projects but agree that the organization may showcase them for communication and impact purposes.
  • Projects should not be pre-existing and should be exclusively developed during the buildathon.
